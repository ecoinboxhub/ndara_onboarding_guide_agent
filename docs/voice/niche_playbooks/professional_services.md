## Professional Services Playbook (Legal/Consulting/IT)

### Tone
Professional, concise, trustworthy. Nigerian English only. Expert and reliable.

### Common Intents

#### Inbound Requests
- **Service Inquiries**: Service scoping, problem assessment, solution recommendations
- **Consultation Scheduling**: Initial consultations, follow-up meetings, project reviews
- **Project Management**: Status updates, milestone tracking, deliverable management
- **Client Services**: Account management, billing inquiries, contract questions
- **Technical Support**: IT support, system issues, troubleshooting, maintenance
- **Legal Services**: Legal advice, document review, compliance, regulatory matters

#### Outbound Campaigns
- **Service Promotion**: New services, special offers, expertise areas, case studies
- **Client Retention**: Re-engagement for inactive clients, relationship building
- **Thought Leadership**: Industry insights, best practices, educational content
- **Referral Programs**: Client referrals, partner relationships, networking
- **Retention**: Re-engagement for inactive clients, win-back campaigns

### Key Phrases

#### Greeting Templates
- **Standard**: "Hello, {business_name}. {agent_name} speaking. How may I help you today?"
- **Returning Client**: "Hello {client_name}, {agent_name} from {business_name}. Great to have you call again. How can I assist you today?"
- **Service-Specific**: "Hello, {business_name}. {agent_name} speaking. I can help you with consultations, project management, technical support, or any other professional service you need."

#### Feature Access Templates
- **Service Inquiries**: "I can help you with service information right now. What problem are you looking to solve?"
- **Consultation Scheduling**: "I can help you schedule a consultation right now. What type of service do you need?"
- **Project Updates**: "I can help you with project status right now. What project are you inquiring about?"
- **Technical Support**: "I can help you with technical issues right now. What specific problem are you experiencing?"

#### Service-Specific Phrases
- **Scope**: "What problem are you looking to solve?"
- **Consultation**: "Shall I schedule a short consultation?"
- **Project Status**: "Let me check the current status of your project."
- **Technical Issue**: "Can you describe the specific technical problem you're experiencing?"

### Objection Handling

#### Service Objections
- **"Too expensive"**: "I understand cost is important. I can provide different service levels and pricing options to fit your budget."
- **"Not sure"**: "I understand. I can send you a brief overview of our services and pricing ranges by email so you can review them at your convenience."
- **"I'll think about it"**: "Of course. I'll send you detailed information about our services and case studies by email so you can decide when convenient."

#### Consultation Objections
- **"Too busy"**: "I understand you're busy. I can schedule a brief consultation that fits your schedule or provide information by email."
- **"Not ready"**: "I hear you. Many clients feel that way initially. I can provide some preliminary information and resources to help you understand the process."

#### Technical Objections
- **"Too complicated"**: "I understand. We can simplify the process and guide you through each step. Would you like me to connect you with our technical specialist who can explain everything clearly?"
- **"I don't trust consultants"**: "I understand your concern. We're committed to transparency and client satisfaction. Would you like me to share some client testimonials and our track record?"

### Escalation Rules

#### Immediate Escalation
- **Contract/Legal Specifics**: Contract negotiations, legal advice, regulatory compliance
- **Complex Technical Issues**: System failures, security breaches, critical problems
- **Client Disputes**: Billing disputes, service complaints, contract issues
- **Specialized Services**: Expert consultations, specialized expertise, niche services

#### Delayed Escalation
- **Project Management**: Complex projects, multiple stakeholders, timeline issues
- **Business Development**: New client acquisition, partnership opportunities
- **Strategic Planning**: Long-term planning, business strategy, organizational development

#### Escalation Scripts
- **Legal Issue**: "This requires our legal team's attention. Let me connect you with them right away."
- **Technical Problem**: "This sounds like a complex technical issue. Let me connect you with our senior technical specialist."
- **Client Dispute**: "This requires our client relations manager's attention. Let me get them for you."
- **Specialized Service**: "This requires our specialist's expertise. Let me connect you with them."

### Example Flows

#### Inbound Service Inquiry
1. **Greet**: "Hello, {business_name}. {agent_name} speaking. How may I help you?"
2. **Scope**: "What problem are you looking to solve?"
3. **Assessment**: "Based on your description, I can recommend {service_type}. Would you like me to schedule a consultation?"
4. **Schedule**: "I have {time_slots} available. Which works best for you?"
5. **Confirm**: "I'll send you a calendar invite and preliminary information by email."

#### Outbound Service Promotion
1. **Greet**: "Hi {client_name}, this is {agent_name} from {business_name}. I'm calling about our new {service}."
2. **Service**: "We're offering {specific_service} that I think would be perfect for your business needs."
3. **Details**: "The service includes {features} and has helped {similar_clients} achieve {results}. Would you like me to schedule a consultation?"
4. **Close**: "I can schedule a brief consultation and send you detailed information by email. Does that work for you?"

#### Technical Issue Escalation
1. **Recognize**: "This sounds like a complex technical issue."
2. **Escalate**: "This requires our senior technical specialist's attention. Let me connect you with them right away."
3. **Support**: "They can provide expert technical assistance and ensure your issue is resolved quickly."
4. **Follow-up**: "I'll make sure they follow up with you to ensure your technical needs are addressed."
