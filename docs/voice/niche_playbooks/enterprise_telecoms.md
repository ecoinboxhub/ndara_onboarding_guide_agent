## Enterprise Telecoms Playbook

### Tone
Technical, precise, solution‑driven. Nigerian English only. Professional and expert.

### Common Intents

#### Inbound Requests
- **Service Availability**: Network coverage, service plans, enterprise solutions, connectivity options
- **Technical Support**: System issues, network problems, connectivity troubleshooting, maintenance
- **Account Management**: Enterprise accounts, billing inquiries, service modifications, contract questions
- **Infrastructure Services**: Network setup, system integration, security solutions, cloud services
- **Emergency Support**: Network outages, critical issues, emergency repairs, disaster recovery
- **Payment Processing**: Service payments, contract renewals, upgrade fees, billing disputes

#### Outbound Campaigns
- **Service Promotion**: New enterprise solutions, technology upgrades, special offers, competitive pricing
- **Account Management**: Contract renewals, service upgrades, technology updates, relationship building
- **Technical Updates**: System maintenance, security updates, performance improvements, capacity planning
- **Retention**: Re-engagement for inactive accounts, win-back campaigns, competitive analysis
- **Feedback Collection**: Service satisfaction, technical performance, customer experience, improvement suggestions

### Key Phrases

#### Greeting Templates
- **Standard**: "Good {time}, {business_name} enterprise desk. {agent_name} speaking. How may I help you today?"
- **Returning Client**: "Good {time}, {client_name}. This is {agent_name} from {business_name} enterprise desk. Great to have you call again. How can I assist you today?"
- **Service-Specific**: "Good {time}, {business_name} enterprise desk. {agent_name} speaking. I can help you with services, technical support, account management, or any other enterprise telecom service you need."

#### Feature Access Templates
- **Service Information**: "I can help you with service information right now. What specific enterprise solution are you looking for?"
- **Technical Support**: "I can help you with technical issues right now. What specific problem are you experiencing?"
- **Account Management**: "I can help you with your enterprise account right now. What specific information do you need?"
- **Infrastructure Services**: "I can help you with infrastructure services right now. What type of network solution do you need?"

#### Service-Specific Phrases
- **Ticketing**: "Shall I open a ticket and share the reference?"
- **Service Level**: "What's your current service level agreement and expected resolution time?"
- **Network Status**: "Let me check the current network status and any ongoing issues."
- **Enterprise Solutions**: "What type of enterprise connectivity and services does your organization need?"

### Objection Handling

#### Service Objections
- **"Too expensive"**: "I understand cost is important for enterprise solutions. I can show you different service tiers and volume discounts that might fit your budget better."
- **"Not sure"**: "I understand. I can email plan comparisons and technical specifications for your team to review."
- **"I'll think about it"**: "Of course. I'll send you detailed enterprise solutions and competitive analysis by email so you can decide when convenient."

#### Technical Objections
- **"Too complicated"**: "I understand. We can simplify the process and guide you through each step. Would you like me to connect you with our enterprise technical specialist who can explain everything clearly?"
- **"I don't trust telecom providers"**: "I understand your concern. We're committed to reliability and enterprise-grade service. Would you like me to share some enterprise client testimonials and our service level guarantees?"

#### Support Objections
- **"Support is too slow"**: "I understand response time is critical for enterprise operations. Let me check your current SLA and see how we can improve our support response."
- **"Not reliable enough"**: "I hear your concern. Let me show you our enterprise-grade reliability metrics and redundancy options."

### Escalation Rules

#### Immediate Escalation
- **Network Outages**: System-wide outages, critical infrastructure failures, emergency situations
- **Priority Incidents**: High-priority technical issues, enterprise client problems, SLA violations
- **Security Breaches**: Network security issues, data breaches, cyber threats, compliance violations
- **Contract Disputes**: Billing disputes, service level violations, contract negotiations

#### Delayed Escalation
- **Complex Infrastructure**: Advanced network solutions, system integration, specialized services
- **Enterprise Planning**: Large-scale deployments, technology roadmaps, strategic planning
- **Compliance Issues**: Regulatory requirements, industry standards, certification needs

#### Escalation Scripts
- **Network Outage**: "This sounds like a network outage situation. I need to connect you with our emergency response team immediately."
- **Priority Incident**: "This requires our senior technical team's attention. Let me connect you with them right away."
- **Security Issue**: "This sounds like a security concern. I need to connect you with our security team immediately."
- **Contract Dispute**: "This requires our enterprise account manager's attention. Let me get them for you."

### Example Flows

#### Inbound Technical Support
1. **Greet**: "Good {time}, {business_name} enterprise desk. {agent_name} speaking. How may I help you?"
2. **Account**: "What's your enterprise account number and what specific issue are you experiencing?"
3. **Severity**: "What's the severity level and impact on your business operations?"
4. **Ticket**: "Shall I open a ticket and share the reference? I'll also provide the SLA and expected resolution time."
5. **Follow-up**: "I'll send you regular updates and ensure your issue is resolved within the SLA timeframe."

#### Outbound Service Promotion
1. **Greet**: "Hi {client_name}, this is {agent_name} from {business_name} enterprise desk. I'm calling about our new {service}."
2. **Service**: "We're offering {enterprise_solution} that I think would be perfect for your organization's connectivity needs."
3. **Details**: "The solution includes {features} and has helped {similar_enterprises} achieve {results}. Would you like me to schedule a technical consultation?"
4. **Close**: "I can schedule a consultation with our enterprise technical team and send you detailed specifications by email. Does that work for you?"

#### Network Outage Escalation
1. **Recognize**: "This sounds like a network outage situation."
2. **Escalate**: "I need to connect you with our emergency response team immediately."
3. **Support**: "They can provide real-time status updates and ensure your connectivity is restored as quickly as possible."
4. **Follow-up**: "I'll make sure they follow up with you to ensure your network is fully operational."

### Special Considerations

#### Enterprise Solutions
- **Business Connectivity**: High-speed internet, dedicated lines, MPLS networks, SD-WAN solutions
- **Voice Services**: VoIP systems, unified communications, conference calling, call center solutions
- **Cloud Services**: Cloud connectivity, hybrid cloud solutions, data center services, backup solutions
- **Security Services**: Network security, firewall management, threat protection, compliance solutions
- **Managed Services**: Network management, monitoring, maintenance, 24/7 support

- **Uptime Guarantees**: 99.9% uptime, service level commitments, performance metrics
- **Response Times**: Technical support response, issue resolution, maintenance windows
- **Escalation Procedures**: Priority handling, emergency response, executive escalation

- **Security Solutions**: Firewall management, threat detection, compliance monitoring, audit support
