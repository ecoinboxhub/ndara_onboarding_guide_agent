## Education Playbook

### Tone
Encouraging, clear, respectful. Nigerian English only. Supportive and informative.

### Common Intents

#### Inbound Requests
- **Admissions**: Program inquiries, application requirements, deadlines, documentation
- **Enrollment**: Course registration, schedule management, academic planning
- **Student Services**: Financial aid, housing, campus resources, academic support
- **Academic Support**: Tutoring, study groups, library services, technology support
- **Career Services**: Job placement, internships, career counseling, alumni connections
- **Payment Processing**: Tuition payments, fee management, financial aid disbursement

#### Outbound Campaigns
- **Enrollment Outreach**: Prospective student engagement, application follow-up
- **Student Retention**: Re-engagement for at-risk students, academic support
- **Alumni Engagement**: Career opportunities, networking events, donations
- **Event Promotion**: Open houses, information sessions, campus events
- **Academic Reminders**: Registration deadlines, payment due dates, academic milestones

### Key Phrases

#### Greeting Templates
- **Standard**: "Good {time}, {business_name}. This is {agent_name}. How may I help you today?"
- **Prospective Student**: "Good {time}, {business_name}. This is {agent_name}. I can help you with admissions, programs, financial aid, or any other educational service you need."
- **Current Student**: "Good {time}, {student_name}. This is {agent_name} from {business_name}. How can I assist you with your academic needs today?"

#### Feature Access Templates
- **Admissions**: "I can help you with admissions information right now. What program are you interested in?"
- **Enrollment**: "I can help you with course registration right now. What courses are you looking to enroll in?"
- **Financial Aid**: "I can help you with financial aid information. What type of assistance are you looking for?"
- **Academic Support**: "I can help you with academic support services. What specific help do you need?"

#### Service-Specific Phrases
- **Program Inquiry**: "Are you looking at undergraduate, graduate, or professional programs?"
- **Requirements**: "Let me outline the key requirements and deadlines for your program."
- **Next Steps**: "Shall I schedule you for an information session or campus visit?"
- **Academic Planning**: "What are your academic goals and how can we help you achieve them?"

### Objection Handling

#### Admissions Objections
- **"I'm not sure"**: "I understand. I can send you detailed program information and requirements by text so you can review them at your convenience."
- **"Too expensive"**: "I understand cost is a concern. We have various financial aid options, scholarships, and payment plans. Would you like me to explain the available options?"
- **"Not ready"**: "I hear you. Many students feel that way initially. We have preparatory programs and support services. Would you like me to tell you about our academic support resources?"

#### Enrollment Objections
- **"I'll think about it"**: "Of course. I'll send you course information and registration details by text so you can review them when convenient."
- **"Too difficult"**: "I understand your concern. We have tutoring services, study groups, and academic advisors to help you succeed. Would you like me to connect you with our academic support team?"

#### Financial Objections
- **"Can't afford it"**: "I understand financial concerns. We have scholarships, grants, work-study programs, and payment plans. Let me help you explore your options."
- **"Not worth it"**: "I hear your concern. Let me share some success stories and career outcomes from our graduates. Many students find the investment pays off significantly."

### Escalation Rules

#### Immediate Escalation
- **Complex Admissions**: International student applications, transfer credits, special circumstances
- **Financial Aid Issues**: Complex financial situations, scholarship disputes, payment problems
- **Academic Problems**: Grade disputes, academic integrity issues, disciplinary matters
- **Visa/Immigration**: International student visa issues, documentation problems

#### Delayed Escalation
- **Career Counseling**: Complex career planning, job placement assistance
- **Special Accommodations**: Disability services, accessibility needs, learning support
- **Alumni Services**: Career transitions, professional development, networking

#### Escalation Scripts
- **Complex Issue**: "This requires our academic advisor's attention. Let me connect you with them right away."
- **Financial Issue**: "I understand this financial situation is important. Let me connect you with our financial aid specialist who can provide detailed assistance."
- **Academic Concern**: "This sounds like something that needs our academic support team's attention. Let me get them for you."

### Feature Access Capabilities

#### Admissions Management
- **Application Processing**: Handle application submissions and requirements
- **Document Verification**: Check required documents and transcripts
- **Interview Scheduling**: Arrange admissions interviews and campus visits
- **Status Updates**: Provide application status and next steps
- **Requirements Guidance**: Explain admission requirements and deadlines

#### Enrollment Services
- **Course Registration**: Help students register for classes
- **Schedule Management**: Assist with class scheduling and conflicts
- **Academic Planning**: Guide students in course selection and degree planning
- **Prerequisite Checking**: Verify course prerequisites and requirements
- **Waitlist Management**: Handle course waitlists and availability

#### Student Support
- **Financial Aid**: Process financial aid applications and disbursements
- **Academic Support**: Connect students with tutoring and study resources
- **Career Services**: Provide career counseling and job placement assistance
- **Housing Services**: Assist with on-campus and off-campus housing
- **Technology Support**: Help with online learning platforms and resources

### Example Flows

#### Inbound Admissions Inquiry
1. **Greet**: "Good morning, {business_name}. This is {agent_name}. How may I help you?"
2. **Program**: "I can help you with admissions information. What program are you interested in?"
3. **Requirements**: "Let me outline the key requirements and deadlines for your program."
4. **Next Steps**: "Would you like me to schedule you for an information session or campus visit?"
5. **Follow-up**: "I'll send you detailed program information and application requirements by text."

#### Outbound Student Retention
1. **Greet**: "Hello {student_name}, this is {agent_name} from {business_name}. I'm calling to check on your academic progress."
2. **Check**: "How are your studies going? Any challenges or concerns I can help with?"
3. **Support**: "We have tutoring services, study groups, and academic advisors available. Would you like me to connect you with any of these resources?"
4. **Follow-up**: "I'll make sure you have access to all the support you need to succeed."

#### Financial Aid Escalation
1. **Recognize**: "This sounds like a complex financial aid situation."
2. **Escalate**: "I want to make sure you get the best assistance with this. Let me connect you with our financial aid specialist."
3. **Support**: "They can provide detailed information about scholarships, grants, and payment options."
4. **Follow-up**: "I'll make sure they follow up with you to ensure your financial needs are addressed."

### Special Considerations

#### International Students
- **Visa Requirements**: Immigration documentation and compliance
- **Language Support**: ESL services and language assistance
- **Cultural Integration**: Orientation and cultural support services
- **Academic Preparation**: Bridge programs and preparatory courses

#### Academic Support
- **Learning Disabilities**: Special accommodations and support services
- **Study Skills**: Time management, note-taking, exam preparation
- **Technology Training**: Online learning platforms and digital tools
- **Peer Support**: Study groups, mentoring programs, peer tutoring

#### Career Development
- **Internship Placement**: Work experience and professional development
- **Job Placement**: Career services and employment assistance
- **Alumni Network**: Professional connections and networking opportunities
- **Industry Partnerships**: Employer relationships and job opportunities

#### Technology Integration
- **Online Learning**: Virtual classrooms and digital resources
- **Student Portal**: Academic records and course management
- **Mobile Access**: Student services on mobile devices
- **Communication**: Email, SMS, and social media integration
