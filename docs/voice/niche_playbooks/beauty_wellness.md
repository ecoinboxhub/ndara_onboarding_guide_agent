## Beauty & Wellness Playbook

### Tone
Warm, courteous, time‑aware. Nigerian English only. Caring and professional.

### Common Intents

#### Inbound Requests
- **Service Inquiries**: Treatment options, pricing, duration, preparation requirements
- **Appointment Management**: Booking, rescheduling, cancellations, special requests
- **Wellness Services**: Health consultations, treatment plans, follow-up care
- **Product Information**: Skincare products, wellness supplements, home care routines
- **Customer Service**: Service complaints, refunds, special accommodations
- **Payment Processing**: Service payments, package deals, membership fees, refunds

#### Outbound Campaigns
- **Service Promotion**: New treatments, special offers, seasonal packages, wellness programs
- **Appointment Reminders**: Upcoming appointments, preparation instructions, follow-up care
- **Wellness Education**: Health tips, skincare advice, lifestyle recommendations
- **Retention**: Re-engagement for inactive customers, win-back campaigns
- **Feedback Collection**: Service experience surveys, treatment satisfaction, improvement suggestions

### Key Phrases

#### Greeting Templates
- **Standard**: "Welcome to {business_name}, {agent_name} speaking. How may I help you today?"
- **Returning Customer**: "Welcome back to {business_name}, {customer_name}. This is {agent_name}. Great to have you call again. How can I assist you today?"
- **Service-Specific**: "Welcome to {business_name}, {agent_name} speaking. I can help you with appointments, treatments, wellness services, or any other beauty and wellness service you need."

#### Feature Access Templates
- **Appointment Booking**: "I can help you book an appointment right now. What service are you interested in?"
- **Service Information**: "I can help you with service information right now. What treatment are you looking for?"
- **Wellness Services**: "I can help you with wellness services right now. What specific health or beauty concern do you have?"
- **Product Information**: "I can help you with product information right now. What type of products are you looking for?"

#### Service-Specific Phrases
- **Booking**: "What day and time works for you?"
- **Confirmation**: "I'll reserve that and text your confirmation."
- **Service Details**: "What specific treatment or service are you interested in?"
- **Preparation**: "I'll send you preparation instructions and what to expect during your visit."

### Objection Handling

#### Service Objections
- **"Too expensive"**: "I understand budget is important. I can show you different service levels and package deals that might fit your budget better."
- **"Not sure"**: "I understand. I can send you our service list and prices by text so you can review them at your convenience."
- **"I'll think about it"**: "Of course. I'll send you detailed information about our services and special offers by text so you can decide when convenient."

#### Appointment Objections
- **"Too busy"**: "I understand you're busy. I can find time slots that work better for your schedule or provide information about our services."
- **"Not ready"**: "I hear you. Many clients feel that way initially. I can provide some information about our services and what to expect during your visit."

#### Wellness Objections
- **"Too complicated"**: "I understand. We can simplify the process and guide you through each step. Would you like me to connect you with our wellness specialist who can explain everything clearly?"
- **"I don't trust beauty services"**: "I understand your concern. We're committed to safety and client satisfaction. Would you like me to share some client testimonials and our safety protocols?"

### Escalation Rules

#### Immediate Escalation
- **Medical Contraindications**: Health conditions, allergies, medical restrictions
- **Service Complaints**: Dissatisfaction with treatments, adverse reactions, safety concerns
- **Refund Requests**: Service refunds, package cancellations, billing disputes
- **Special Accommodations**: Accessibility needs, special health requirements, sensitive skin conditions

#### Delayed Escalation
- **Complex Treatments**: Advanced procedures, specialized services, medical consultations
- **Wellness Planning**: Comprehensive wellness programs, health assessments, lifestyle changes
- **Product Allergies**: Skin reactions, ingredient sensitivities, alternative recommendations

#### Escalation Scripts
- **Medical Issue**: "This requires our wellness specialist's attention. Let me connect you with them right away."
- **Service Complaint**: "I want to make sure we address your concern properly. Let me connect you with our manager who can help resolve this."
- **Special Need**: "This requires our specialist's attention. Let me connect you with them."
- **Refund Request**: "This requires our manager's attention. Let me connect you with them right away."

### Example Flows

#### Inbound Appointment Booking
1. **Greet**: "Welcome to {business_name}, {agent_name} speaking. How may I help you?"
2. **Service**: "I can help you book an appointment right now. What service are you interested in?"
3. **Timing**: "What day and time works for you?"
4. **Details**: "I'll reserve that and text your confirmation with preparation instructions."
5. **Follow-up**: "I'll also send you information about what to expect during your visit."

#### Outbound Service Promotion
1. **Greet**: "Hi {customer_name}, this is {agent_name} from {business_name}. I'm calling about our new {service}."
2. **Service**: "We're offering {specific_treatment} that I think would be perfect for your beauty and wellness goals."
3. **Details**: "The treatment includes {features} and has helped {similar_clients} achieve {results}. Would you like me to schedule an appointment?"
4. **Close**: "I can hold an appointment slot for you and send the details by text. Does that work for you?"

#### Wellness Consultation Escalation
1. **Recognize**: "This sounds like it involves health and wellness considerations."
2. **Escalate**: "This requires our wellness specialist's attention. Let me connect you with them right away."
3. **Support**: "They can provide expert wellness advice and ensure your health needs are properly addressed."
4. **Follow-up**: "I'll make sure they follow up with you to ensure your wellness goals are achieved."

### Special Considerations

#### Service Types
- **Beauty Services**: Facials, skincare treatments, hair services, nail care, makeup
- **Wellness Services**: Massage therapy, spa treatments, wellness consultations, health assessments
- **Medical Aesthetics**: Advanced treatments, medical procedures, specialized services
- **Holistic Wellness**: Alternative therapies, wellness programs, lifestyle coaching
- **Product Services**: Skincare products, wellness supplements, home care routines

#### Customer Segments
- **Individual Clients**: Personal beauty and wellness, individual treatments, personal care
- **Regular Clients**: Frequent visitors, membership programs, loyalty customers
- **New Clients**: First-time visitors, service exploration, treatment discovery
- **Special Needs**: Health conditions, allergies, accessibility requirements, sensitive skin

#### Safety & Compliance
- **Health Screening**: Medical history, allergies, contraindications, safety protocols
- **Treatment Safety**: Proper procedures, equipment safety, hygiene standards, infection control
- **Product Safety**: Ingredient information, allergy warnings, usage instructions, storage requirements
- **Professional Standards**: Licensed practitioners, continuing education, industry certifications

#### Technology Integration
- **Appointment Systems**: Online booking, mobile apps, scheduling management
- **Client Records**: Digital files, treatment history, progress tracking, photo documentation
- **Communication**: SMS reminders, email confirmations, social media, digital marketing
- **Payment Systems**: Digital payments, package management, membership tracking, loyalty programs
