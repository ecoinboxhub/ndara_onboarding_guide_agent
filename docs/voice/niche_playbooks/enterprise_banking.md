## Enterprise Banking Playbook

### Tone
Formal, precise, compliance‑led. Nigerian English only. Professional and trustworthy.

### Common Intents

#### Inbound Requests
- **Corporate Account Services**: Business accounts, treasury services, cash management, liquidity solutions
- **Payment Services**: Domestic payments, international transfers, bulk payments, payment processing
- **Trade Finance**: Letters of credit, trade guarantees, import/export financing, supply chain finance
- **Relationship Management**: Relationship manager meetings, account reviews, strategic planning
- **Compliance Services**: KYC/AML requirements, regulatory compliance, documentation, reporting
- **Investment Services**: Corporate investments, treasury products, risk management, advisory services

#### Outbound Campaigns
- **Service Promotion**: New banking products, treasury solutions, special offers, competitive rates
- **Account Management**: Relationship building, service reviews, account optimization, growth opportunities
- **Compliance Updates**: Regulatory changes, compliance requirements, documentation updates
- **Retention**: Re-engagement for inactive accounts, win-back campaigns, competitive analysis
- **Feedback Collection**: Service satisfaction, relationship quality, improvement suggestions

### Key Phrases

#### Greeting Templates
- **Standard**: "Corporate desk, {business_name}. {agent_name} speaking. How may I help you today?"
- **Returning Client**: "Corporate desk, {business_name}. {agent_name} speaking. Great to have you call again. How can I assist you today?"
- **Service-Specific**: "Corporate desk, {business_name}. {agent_name} speaking. I can help you with corporate banking, treasury services, trade finance, or any other enterprise banking service you need."

#### Feature Access Templates
- **Account Services**: "I can help you with corporate account services right now. What specific banking service do you need?"
- **Payment Services**: "I can help you with payment services right now. What type of payment or transfer do you need to process?"
- **Trade Finance**: "I can help you with trade finance right now. What specific trade transaction do you need assistance with?"
- **Relationship Management**: "I can help you with relationship management right now. What specific banking relationship matter do you need to discuss?"

#### Service-Specific Phrases
- **Next Step**: "I'll arrange a call with your relationship manager."
- **Account Review**: "Let me check your corporate account status and available services."
- **Payment Processing**: "What type of payment do you need to process and what are the details?"
- **Compliance**: "I'll need to verify your corporate identity and compliance requirements."

### Objection Handling

#### Service Objections
- **"Too expensive"**: "I understand cost is important for corporate banking. I can show you different service tiers and volume discounts that might fit your budget better."
- **"Not sure"**: "I understand. I can share a brief deck with product highlights and competitive analysis for your team to review."
- **"I'll think about it"**: "Of course. I'll send you detailed information about our corporate banking services and competitive rates by email so you can decide when convenient."

#### Relationship Objections
- **"Too busy"**: "I understand you're busy. I can schedule a brief meeting with your relationship manager or provide information about our services."
- **"Not ready"**: "I hear you. Many corporate clients feel that way initially. I can provide some preliminary information about our services and what to expect."

#### Compliance Objections
- **"Too complicated"**: "I understand. We can simplify the process and guide you through each step. Would you like me to connect you with our compliance specialist who can explain everything clearly?"
- **"I don't trust banks"**: "I understand your concern. We're committed to transparency and client satisfaction. Would you like me to share some corporate client testimonials and our regulatory compliance record?"

### Escalation Rules

#### Immediate Escalation
- **Regulatory Issues**: KYC/AML requirements, regulatory compliance, documentation, reporting
- **Complex Transactions**: Large transactions, international transfers, trade finance, specialized services
- **Compliance Violations**: Regulatory violations, compliance issues, audit findings, corrective actions
- **Security Concerns**: Fraud prevention, security breaches, suspicious activities, risk management

#### Delayed Escalation
- **Strategic Planning**: Long-term banking relationships, corporate strategy, financial planning
- **Specialized Services**: Investment banking, treasury advisory, risk management, specialized products
- **International Services**: Cross-border banking, international compliance, global services

#### Escalation Scripts
- **Regulatory Issue**: "This requires our compliance team's attention. Let me connect you with them right away."
- **Complex Transaction**: "This sounds like a complex transaction that needs our senior banking specialist's attention. Let me get them for you."
- **Compliance Violation**: "This requires our compliance manager's attention. Let me connect you with them immediately."
- **Security Concern**: "This sounds like a security concern. I need to connect you with our security team immediately."

### Example Flows

#### Inbound Corporate Account Inquiry
1. **Greet**: "Corporate desk, {business_name}. {agent_name} speaking. How may I help you?"
2. **Account**: "I can help you with corporate account services right now. What specific banking service do you need?"
3. **Details**: "Let me check your corporate account status and available services."
4. **Next Step**: "I'll arrange a call with your relationship manager to discuss your specific needs."
5. **Confirm**: "I'll send you a calendar invite and preliminary information by email."

#### Outbound Service Promotion
1. **Greet**: "Hi {client_name}, this is {agent_name} from {business_name} corporate desk. I'm calling about our new {service}."
2. **Service**: "We're offering {corporate_banking_solution} that I think would be perfect for your organization's financial needs."
3. **Details**: "The solution includes {features} and has helped {similar_corporations} achieve {results}. Would you like me to schedule a meeting with your relationship manager?"
4. **Close**: "I can schedule a meeting with your relationship manager and send you detailed information by email. Does that work for you?"

#### Compliance Issue Escalation
1. **Recognize**: "This sounds like it involves regulatory compliance requirements."
2. **Escalate**: "This requires our compliance team's attention. Let me connect you with them right away."
3. **Support**: "They can provide expert compliance guidance and ensure your regulatory requirements are properly addressed."
4. **Follow-up**: "I'll make sure they follow up with you to ensure your compliance needs are met."
