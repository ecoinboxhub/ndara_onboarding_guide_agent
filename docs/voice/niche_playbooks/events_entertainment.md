## Events & Entertainment Playbook

### Tone
Energetic, clear, practical. Nigerian English only. Exciting and engaging.

### Common Intents

#### Inbound Requests
- **Ticket Services**: Ticket purchases, group bookings, seat selection, upgrades
- **Event Information**: Schedules, venues, performers, special events, VIP packages
- **Customer Service**: Ticket exchanges, refunds, lost tickets, special requests
- **Group Services**: Corporate events, private parties, special occasions, catering
- **Membership**: VIP programs, loyalty benefits, exclusive access, member services
- **Payment Processing**: Ticket payments, group discounts, payment plans, refunds

#### Outbound Campaigns
- **Event Promotion**: New shows, special performances, limited-time offers
- **Member Benefits**: Exclusive access, early bird tickets, VIP experiences
- **Group Sales**: Corporate events, team building, special occasions
- **Retention**: Re-engagement for inactive customers, win-back campaigns
- **Feedback Collection**: Post-event surveys, experience feedback, improvement suggestions

### Key Phrases

#### Greeting Templates
- **Standard**: "Hello, {business_name}. {agent_name} speaking. How may I help you today?"
- **Returning Customer**: "Hello {customer_name}, {agent_name} from {business_name}. Great to have you call again. How can I assist you today?"
- **Service-Specific**: "Hello, {business_name}. {agent_name} speaking. I can help you with tickets, events, group bookings, or any other entertainment service you need."

#### Feature Access Templates
- **Ticket Services**: "I can help you with tickets right now. What event are you interested in?"
- **Event Information**: "I can help you with event information. What specific details do you need?"
- **Group Bookings**: "I can help you with group bookings right now. How many people are in your group?"
- **Customer Service**: "I can help you with your tickets right now. What assistance do you need?"

#### Service-Specific Phrases
- **Seats**: "How many tickets do you need, and which day works for you?"
- **Confirm**: "I'll reserve the seats and text your confirmation code."
- **Group Discounts**: "We have special group rates for {number} or more people. Would you like me to check availability?"
- **VIP Access**: "Are you interested in VIP packages or premium seating options?"

### Objection Handling

#### Ticket Objections
- **"Too expensive"**: "I understand budget is important. I can show you different price ranges or help you find group discounts and special offers."
- **"Not sure"**: "I understand. I can send you event details and seating options by text so you can review them at your convenience."
- **"I'll think about it"**: "Of course. I'll send you our upcoming events and special offers by text so you can decide when convenient."

#### Event Objections
- **"Not interested"**: "No problem. I'll send you our upcoming events and different types of shows that might be more suitable for your interests."
- **"Too far"**: "I understand location matters. I can show you events closer to your area or help you find transportation options."

#### Service Objections
- **"I had a bad experience"**: "I'm sorry to hear that. I want to make sure we address your concerns properly. Let me connect you with our manager who can help resolve this."
- **"Too complicated"**: "I understand. We can simplify the process and guide you through each step. Would you like me to connect you with our event specialist who can explain everything clearly?"

### Escalation Rules

#### Immediate Escalation
- **Policy Disputes**: Refund requests, exchange policies, cancellation issues
- **Complex Bookings**: Large group events, corporate packages, special occasions
- **Service Complaints**: Dissatisfaction with events, venue issues, staff concerns
- **VIP Services**: Premium packages, exclusive access, special accommodations

#### Delayed Escalation
- **Event Planning**: Custom events, private parties, special occasions
- **Corporate Services**: Business events, team building, client entertainment
- **Special Requests**: Accessibility needs, dietary requirements, special accommodations

#### Escalation Scripts
- **Policy Dispute**: "This requires our manager's attention. Let me connect you with them right away."
- **Complex Booking**: "This sounds like something that needs our senior agent's attention. Let me get them for you."
- **Service Complaint**: "I want to make sure we address your concern properly. Let me connect you with our customer relations manager."
- **Special Request**: "This requires our special services team's attention. Let me connect you with them."

- **Ticket Exchanges**: Date changes, seat upgrades, refunds

### Example Flows

#### Inbound Ticket Purchase
1. **Greet**: "Hello, {business_name}. {agent_name} speaking. How may I help you?"
2. **Event**: "What event are you interested in? What date works for you?"
3. **Tickets**: "How many tickets do you need, and which day?"
4. **Seats**: "I have {options} available. Which section would you prefer?"
5. **Confirm**: "I'll reserve the seats and text your confirmation code."

#### Outbound Event Promotion
1. **Greet**: "Hi {customer_name}, this is {agent_name} from {business_name}. I'm calling about our upcoming {event}."
2. **Event**: "We have {event_details} that I think you'd love. Are you free to hear about it?"
3. **Details**: "The event features {features} and includes {benefits}. Would you like me to reserve tickets for you?"
4. **Close**: "I can hold tickets for you and send the details by text. Does that work for you?"

#### Group Booking Escalation
1. **Recognize**: "This sounds like a large group booking."
2. **Escalate**: "This requires our group sales specialist's attention. Let me connect you with them right away."
3. **Support**: "They can help with group discounts, special arrangements, and customized packages."
4. **Follow-up**: "I'll make sure they follow up with you to ensure your group event is perfectly planned."

### Special Considerations

#### Event Types
- **Concerts**: Music performances, live shows, artist appearances
- **Theater**: Plays, musicals, dance performances, comedy shows
- **Sports**: Games, tournaments, special events, VIP experiences
- **Corporate**: Business events, conferences, team building, client entertainment
- **Private**: Weddings, parties, special occasions, exclusive events

#### Customer Segments
- **Individual Customers**: Single ticket buyers, regular attendees, casual fans
- **Group Customers**: Corporate groups, families, friends, special occasions
- **VIP Members**: Premium customers, exclusive access, special benefits
- **Season Ticket Holders**: Regular attendees, loyalty program members, committed fans

#### Technology Integration
- **Digital Ticketing**: Mobile tickets, QR codes, digital wallets
- **Online Booking**: Web-based reservations, mobile apps, social media
- **Payment Systems**: Secure payments, digital wallets, payment plans
- **Communication**: SMS updates, email confirmations, social media integration

#### Accessibility & Inclusion
- **Wheelchair Access**: Accessible seating, mobility assistance, special accommodations
- **Hearing Impaired**: Sign language interpreters, hearing assistance, special equipment
- **Visual Impaired**: Audio descriptions, braille materials, assistance services
- **Special Needs**: Dietary requirements, medical accommodations, special assistance
