## Manufacturing & FMCG Playbook

### Tone
Practical, supply‑chain aware, concise. Nigerian English only. Professional and efficient.

### Common Intents

#### Inbound Requests
- **Product Availability**: Stock levels, lead times, production schedules, inventory management
- **Pricing & Orders**: Product pricing, minimum order quantities, volume discounts, order processing
- **Order Management**: Order status, delivery tracking, logistics, shipping updates
- **Quality Control**: Product quality, quality issues, returns, quality assurance
- **Supply Chain**: Supplier management, logistics, inventory, production planning
- **Customer Service**: Product support, technical assistance, customer complaints, account management

#### Outbound Campaigns
- **Product Promotion**: New products, special offers, seasonal promotions, product launches
- **Order Management**: Order confirmations, delivery updates, payment reminders, account reviews
- **Quality Updates**: Quality improvements, product updates, safety notifications, compliance updates
- **Retention**: Re-engagement for inactive customers, win-back campaigns, competitive analysis
- **Feedback Collection**: Product satisfaction, service quality, improvement suggestions

### Key Phrases

#### Greeting Templates
- **Standard**: "Good {time}, {business_name}. {agent_name} speaking. How may I help you today?"
- **Returning Customer**: "Good {time}, {customer_name}. This is {agent_name} from {business_name}. Great to have you call again. How can I assist you today?"
- **Service-Specific**: "Good {time}, {business_name}. {agent_name} speaking. I can help you with products, orders, quality, or any other manufacturing and FMCG service you need."

#### Feature Access Templates
- **Product Information**: "I can help you with product information right now. What specific product are you looking for?"
- **Order Management**: "I can help you with your order right now. What order number or product are you inquiring about?"
- **Quality Issues**: "I can help you with quality issues right now. What specific quality concern do you have?"
- **Supply Chain**: "I can help you with supply chain information right now. What specific logistics or inventory matter do you need assistance with?"

#### Service-Specific Phrases
- **Lead Time**: "Current lead time is approximately {X} days."
- **Availability**: "Let me check product availability and current stock levels."
- **Order Status**: "I can track your order status and provide delivery updates."
- **Quality**: "I'll need to verify product quality and any quality issues."

### Objection Handling

#### Product Objections
- **"Too expensive"**: "I understand cost is important for manufacturing and FMCG. I can show you different pricing tiers and volume discounts that might fit your budget better."
- **"Not sure"**: "I understand. I can send a spec sheet and MOQ ranges by email/text so you can review them at your convenience."
- **"I'll think about it"**: "Of course. I'll send you detailed product information and competitive pricing by email so you can decide when convenient."

#### Order Objections
- **"Lead time too long"**: "I understand lead time is important for your operations. I can check for expedited options or alternative products with shorter lead times."
- **"Not available"**: "I understand availability is critical. I can check alternative products or suggest when your preferred product will be available."

#### Quality Objections
- **"Quality issues"**: "I understand quality is important for your business. I can connect you with our quality team who can address your specific concerns and provide solutions."
- **"Too complicated"**: "I understand. We can simplify the process and guide you through each step. Would you like me to connect you with our technical specialist who can explain everything clearly?"

### Escalation Rules

#### Immediate Escalation
- **Quality Disputes**: Product quality issues, quality complaints, quality failures, quality investigations
- **Contractual Terms**: Contract negotiations, terms and conditions, pricing disputes, legal matters
- **Supply Chain Issues**: Production delays, supplier problems, logistics issues, inventory shortages
- **Customer Complaints**: Service complaints, product complaints, delivery issues, account problems

#### Delayed Escalation
- **Complex Orders**: Large volume orders, custom products, specialized requirements, complex logistics
- **Strategic Planning**: Long-term supply agreements, strategic partnerships, business development
- **Technical Issues**: Product specifications, technical requirements, engineering support, specialized products

#### Escalation Scripts
- **Quality Issue**: "This requires our quality team's attention. Let me connect you with them right away."
- **Contract Dispute**: "This requires our sales manager's attention. Let me get them for you."
- **Supply Chain Issue**: "This sounds like a supply chain issue that needs our logistics specialist's attention. Let me connect you with them."
- **Customer Complaint**: "I want to make sure we address your concern properly. Let me connect you with our customer relations manager."

### Example Flows

#### Inbound Product Availability
1. **Greet**: "Good {time}, {business_name}. {agent_name} speaking. How may I help you?"
2. **Product**: "I can help you with product information right now. What specific product are you looking for?"
3. **Availability**: "Let me check product availability and current stock levels."
4. **Lead Time**: "Current lead time is approximately {X} days. I can send you a spec sheet and MOQ ranges by email."
5. **Quote**: "I'll arrange for our sales team to provide a detailed quote and confirm contact details."

#### Outbound Product Promotion
1. **Greet**: "Hi {customer_name}, this is {agent_name} from {business_name}. I'm calling about our new {product}."
2. **Product**: "We're offering {specific_product} that I think would be perfect for your manufacturing or FMCG needs."
3. **Details**: "The product includes {features} and has helped {similar_customers} achieve {results}. Would you like me to send you detailed specifications?"
4. **Close**: "I can send you product information and arrange for our sales team to provide a detailed quote. Does that work for you?"

#### Quality Issue Escalation
1. **Recognize**: "This sounds like a quality issue that needs immediate attention."
2. **Escalate**: "This requires our quality team's attention. Let me connect you with them right away."
3. **Support**: "They can provide expert quality assistance and ensure your quality concerns are properly addressed."
4. **Follow-up**: "I'll make sure they follow up with you to ensure your quality requirements are met."
