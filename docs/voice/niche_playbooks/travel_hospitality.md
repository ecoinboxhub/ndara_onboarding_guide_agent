## Travel & Hospitality Playbook

### Tone
Warm, helpful, efficient. Nigerian English only. Welcoming and service-oriented.

### Common Intents

#### Inbound Requests
- **Booking Services**: Room reservations, flight bookings, package deals, group bookings
- **Travel Planning**: Itinerary planning, destination information, travel advice
- **Customer Service**: Booking modifications, cancellations, special requests, complaints
- **Loyalty Programs**: Membership inquiries, points redemption, benefits, upgrades
- **Amenities & Services**: Hotel services, dining, spa, transportation, concierge
- **Payment Processing**: Booking payments, refunds, loyalty points, gift certificates

#### Outbound Campaigns
- **Promotional Offers**: Special deals, seasonal packages, loyalty member benefits
- **Booking Follow-up**: Post-stay feedback, rebooking incentives, loyalty program updates
- **Event Promotion**: Special events, conferences, weddings, corporate retreats
- **Destination Marketing**: New destinations, travel trends, seasonal promotions
- **Retention**: Re-engagement for inactive customers, win-back campaigns

### Key Phrases

#### Greeting Templates
- **Standard**: "Welcome to {business_name}, {agent_name} speaking. How may I assist you today?"
- **Returning Guest**: "Welcome back to {business_name}, {guest_name}. This is {agent_name}. Great to have you call again. How can I assist you today?"
- **Service-Specific**: "Welcome to {business_name}, {agent_name} speaking. I can help you with bookings, travel planning, loyalty programs, or any other hospitality service you need."

#### Feature Access Templates
- **Booking Services**: "I can help you make a reservation right now. What dates are you looking at?"
- **Travel Planning**: "I can help you plan your trip right now. What destination interests you?"
- **Loyalty Services**: "I can help you with your loyalty account. What specific information do you need?"
- **Customer Service**: "I can help you with your booking right now. What assistance do you need?"

#### Service-Specific Phrases
- **Dates**: "What dates are you looking at? How many guests will be traveling?"
- **Confirmation**: "I'll reserve {option} and text the confirmation details."
- **Special Requests**: "Do you have any special requests or dietary requirements?"
- **Loyalty**: "Are you a member of our loyalty program? I can check your benefits and points."

### Objection Handling

#### Booking Objections
- **"Too expensive"**: "I understand budget is important. I can show you alternative options or help you find special offers and discounts."
- **"Not sure"**: "I understand. I can send you detailed information about our options by text so you can review them at your convenience."
- **"I'll think about it"**: "Of course. I'll send you our best offers and availability by text so you can decide when convenient."

#### Travel Objections
- **"Too far"**: "I understand location matters. I can show you properties closer to your preferred area or help you find transportation options."
- **"Not interested"**: "No problem. I'll send you alternative destinations and packages that might be more suitable for your interests."

#### Service Objections
- **"I had a bad experience"**: "I'm sorry to hear that. I want to make sure we address your concerns properly. Let me connect you with our manager who can help resolve this."
- **"Too complicated"**: "I understand. We can simplify the process and guide you through each step. Would you like me to connect you with our travel specialist who can explain everything clearly?"

### Escalation Rules

#### Immediate Escalation
- **Policy Exceptions**: Refund requests, cancellation exceptions, special accommodations
- **Complex Bookings**: Group bookings, corporate events, special occasions
- **Service Complaints**: Dissatisfaction with service, facility issues, staff concerns
- **Loyalty Issues**: Points disputes, membership problems, benefit disputes

#### Delayed Escalation
- **Special Requests**: Accessibility needs, dietary requirements, medical accommodations
- **Event Planning**: Weddings, conferences, corporate retreats, special occasions
- **Travel Emergencies**: Flight changes, weather issues, travel disruptions

#### Escalation Scripts
- **Policy Exception**: "This requires our manager's attention. Let me connect you with them right away."
- **Complex Booking**: "This sounds like something that needs our senior agent's attention. Let me get them for you."
- **Service Complaint**: "I want to make sure we address your concern properly. Let me connect you with our customer relations manager."
- **Special Request**: "This requires our special services team's attention. Let me connect you with them."

### Example Flows

#### Inbound Booking Request
1. **Greet**: "Welcome to {business_name}, {agent_name} speaking. How may I assist you?"
2. **Dates**: "What dates are you looking at? How many guests will be traveling?"
3. **Preferences**: "Do you have any special requests or preferences?"
4. **Options**: "I have {options} available for your dates. Which would you prefer?"
5. **Confirm**: "I'll reserve {option} and text the confirmation details."

#### Outbound Promotional Call
1. **Greet**: "Hi {guest_name}, this is {agent_name} from {business_name}. I'm calling about our special {promotion}."
2. **Offer**: "We're offering {specific_offer} that I think you'd love. Are you free to hear about it?"
3. **Details**: "The offer includes {details} and is valid until {expiry}. Would you like me to make a reservation for you?"
4. **Close**: "I can hold a booking for you and send the details by text. Does that work for you?"

#### Service Complaint Resolution
1. **Acknowledge**: "I'm sorry to hear about your experience. I want to make sure we address your concerns properly."
2. **Details**: "Can you tell me more about what happened so I can help resolve this?"
3. **Escalate**: "This sounds like something that needs our manager's attention. Let me connect you with them right away."
4. **Follow-up**: "I'll make sure our manager follows up with you to ensure this is resolved to your satisfaction."
