## Logistics & Delivery Playbook

### Tone
Direct, reliable, solution‑oriented. Nigerian English only. Professional and efficient.

### Common Intents

#### Inbound Requests
- **Shipment Tracking**: Package status, delivery ETAs, location updates, delivery confirmations
- **Pickup/Delivery Services**: Schedule pickups, delivery appointments, route optimization
- **Address Management**: Address changes, delivery instructions, location updates
- **Claims & Complaints**: Lost packages, damaged goods, delivery issues, refunds
- **Customer Service**: Delivery problems, special requests, account management
- **Payment Processing**: Shipping payments, insurance, customs fees, refunds

#### Outbound Campaigns
- **Delivery Updates**: Status notifications, delivery confirmations, pickup reminders
- **Service Promotion**: New services, special rates, bulk shipping discounts
- **Customer Retention**: Re-engagement for inactive customers, loyalty programs
- **Feedback Collection**: Delivery experience surveys, service quality, improvement suggestions
- **Retention**: Re-engagement for inactive customers, win-back campaigns

### Key Phrases

#### Greeting Templates
- **Standard**: "Good {time}, {business_name}. {agent_name} speaking. How may I help you today?"
- **Returning Customer**: "Good {time}, {customer_name}. This is {agent_name} from {business_name}. Great to have you call again. How can I assist you today?"
- **Service-Specific**: "Good {time}, {business_name}. {agent_name} speaking. I can help you with tracking, delivery, pickup, or any other logistics service you need."

#### Feature Access Templates
- **Shipment Tracking**: "I can help you track your shipment right now. What's your tracking number?"
- **Delivery Scheduling**: "I can help you schedule a delivery right now. What date and time works for you?"
- **Address Changes**: "I can help you update your address right now. What changes do you need to make?"
- **Claims Processing**: "I can help you with your claim right now. What type of issue are you experiencing?"

#### Service-Specific Phrases
- **Tracking**: "Kindly share your tracking/reference number."
- **Update**: "I'll notify you once it's on the move."
- **Delivery**: "What's your preferred delivery time and date?"
- **Address**: "I'll update your address and confirm the changes."

### Objection Handling

#### Tracking Objections
- **"I don't have the tracking number"**: "No problem. I can look it up using your phone number or address. What's the best way to identify your shipment?"
- **"It's taking too long"**: "I understand your concern. Let me check the current status and see if there are any delays. I'll also set up notifications for you."
- **"I'll check later"**: "Of course. I can text you a tracking link for real-time updates so you can check whenever convenient."

#### Delivery Objections
- **"Not available"**: "I understand. I can reschedule for a time that works better for you. What day and time would be convenient?"
- **"Too expensive"**: "I understand cost is important. I can show you different shipping options or help you find bulk discounts and special rates."

#### Service Objections
- **"I had a bad experience"**: "I'm sorry to hear that. I want to make sure we address your concerns properly. Let me connect you with our manager who can help resolve this."
- **"Too complicated"**: "I understand. We can simplify the process and guide you through each step. Would you like me to connect you with our logistics specialist who can explain everything clearly?"

### Escalation Rules

#### Immediate Escalation
- **Lost/Damaged Claims**: Package loss, damage reports, insurance claims, compensation
- **Delivery Problems**: Failed deliveries, address issues, customer complaints
- **Service Complaints**: Dissatisfaction with service, driver issues, facility problems
- **Complex Issues**: International shipping, customs problems, special handling

#### Delayed Escalation
- **Special Requests**: Fragile items, hazardous materials, special handling
- **Bulk Shipping**: Large volume shipments, corporate accounts, special rates
- **Route Optimization**: Complex delivery routes, multiple stops, efficiency improvements

#### Escalation Scripts
- **Lost Package**: "This requires our claims department's attention. Let me connect you with them right away."
- **Delivery Problem**: "This sounds like something that needs our delivery manager's attention. Let me get them for you."
- **Service Complaint**: "I want to make sure we address your concern properly. Let me connect you with our customer relations manager."
- **Complex Issue**: "This requires our logistics specialist's attention. Let me connect you with them."

### Example Flows

#### Inbound Shipment Tracking
1. **Greet**: "Good {time}, {business_name}. {agent_name} speaking. How may I help you?"
2. **Tracking**: "I can help you track your shipment right now. What's your tracking number?"
3. **Status**: "Your package is currently {status} and expected to arrive by {date}. I'll send you updates by text."
4. **Confirm**: "I'll notify you once it's on the move. Is this the best number to reach you?"

#### Outbound Delivery Update
1. **Greet**: "Hi {customer_name}, this is {agent_name} from {business_name}. I'm calling about your shipment."
2. **Update**: "Your package is scheduled for delivery today between {time_range}. Will you be available to receive it?"
3. **Confirm**: "I'll send you a text confirmation and you can track it in real-time."
4. **Follow-up**: "If you have any questions, just call us back."

#### Lost Package Escalation
1. **Recognize**: "This sounds like a lost package situation."
2. **Escalate**: "This requires our claims department's attention. Let me connect you with them right away."
3. **Support**: "They can help with the claims process and ensure you receive proper compensation."
4. **Follow-up**: "I'll make sure they follow up with you to ensure your claim is processed quickly."

### Special Considerations

#### Delivery Types
- **Standard Delivery**: Regular packages, standard timeframes, basic tracking
- **Express Delivery**: Urgent shipments, same-day delivery, priority handling
- **International**: Cross-border shipping, customs clearance, documentation
- **Specialized**: Fragile items, hazardous materials, oversized packages
- **Bulk**: Corporate shipments, volume discounts, special handling

#### Customer Segments
- **Individual Customers**: Personal shipments, occasional users, standard services
- **Business Customers**: Regular shipments, corporate accounts, volume discounts
- **E-commerce**: Online retailers, marketplace sellers, fulfillment services
- **International**: Cross-border customers, customs requirements, documentation

#### Technology Integration
- **GPS Tracking**: Real-time location updates, route optimization, delivery confirmation
- **Mobile Apps**: Customer apps, driver apps, tracking notifications
- **Digital Platforms**: Online booking, payment processing, account management
- **Communication**: SMS updates, email notifications, automated calls

#### Quality Assurance
- **Delivery Standards**: On-time delivery, package condition, customer satisfaction
- **Driver Training**: Customer service, safety protocols, delivery procedures
- **Performance Monitoring**: Delivery metrics, customer feedback, service quality
