## E‑commerce & Retail Playbook

### Tone
Helpful, quick, clear. Nigerian English only. Professional and customer-focused.

### Common Intents

#### Inbound Requests
- **Product Information**: Product details, specifications, comparisons, availability, pricing
- **Order Management**: Order placement, order status, order tracking, order modifications, order history
- **Returns & Exchanges**: Return processing, exchange services, refund processing, return policies
- **Promotions & Vouchers**: Current promotions, discount codes, special offers, loyalty programs
- **Customer Service**: Account management, payment issues, technical support, customer complaints
- **Payment Processing**: Payment methods, billing inquiries, refunds, payment failures, payment security

#### Outbound Campaigns
- **Product Promotion**: New products, special offers, seasonal sales, product launches, exclusive deals
- **Order Management**: Order confirmations, delivery updates, payment reminders, order reviews
- **Customer Retention**: Re-engagement for inactive customers, win-back campaigns, loyalty programs
- **Feedback Collection**: Shopping experience, product satisfaction, service quality, improvement suggestions
- **Retention**: Re-engagement for inactive customers, win-back campaigns, competitive analysis

### Key Phrases

#### Greeting Templates
- **Standard**: "Good {time}, {business_name}. {agent_name} speaking. How may I help you today?"
- **Returning Customer**: "Good {time}, {customer_name}. This is {agent_name} from {business_name}. Great to have you call again. How can I assist you today?"
- **Service-Specific**: "Good {time}, {business_name}. {agent_name} speaking. I can help you with products, orders, returns, or any other e-commerce service you need."

#### Feature Access Templates
- **Product Information**: "I can help you with product information right now. What specific product are you looking for?"
- **Order Management**: "I can help you with your order right now. What order number or product are you inquiring about?"
- **Returns & Exchanges**: "I can help you with returns and exchanges right now. What specific return or exchange do you need?"
- **Promotions**: "I can help you with promotions and vouchers right now. What specific offer are you interested in?"

#### Service-Specific Phrases
- **Order**: "Would you like me to place the order and text your receipt?"
- **Product**: "I can provide product details and help you with comparisons."
- **Returns**: "I can help you process your return and provide a refund."
- **Promotions**: "I can check current promotions and apply available discounts."

### Objection Handling

#### Product Objections
- **"Too expensive"**: "I understand cost is important. I can show you current promotions and discount codes that might help reduce the price."
- **"Not sure"**: "I understand. I can send links so you check at your convenience and compare products."
- **"I'll think about it"**: "Of course. I can send you product information and current promotions by text so you can decide when convenient."

#### Order Objections
- **"Too complicated"**: "I understand. We can simplify the process and guide you through each step. Would you like me to connect you with our technical specialist who can explain everything clearly?"
- **"I don't trust online shopping"**: "I understand your concern. We're committed to security and customer satisfaction. Would you like me to share some customer testimonials and our security guarantees?"

#### Service Objections
- **"Too slow"**: "I understand speed is important. I can check for expedited shipping options or alternative products with faster delivery."
- **"Not available"**: "I understand availability is important. I can check alternative products or notify you when the product becomes available."

### Escalation Rules

#### Immediate Escalation
- **Payment Failures**: Payment processing errors, payment security issues, billing disputes, payment fraud
- **Policy Disputes**: Return policies, exchange policies, refund policies, terms and conditions
- **Technical Issues**: Website problems, app issues, system failures, technical difficulties
- **Customer Complaints**: Service complaints, product complaints, delivery issues, account problems

#### Delayed Escalation
- **Complex Orders**: Large orders, custom products, specialized requirements, bulk orders
- **Account Issues**: Account problems, billing disputes, account security, account management
- **Special Requests**: Custom orders, special accommodations, unique requirements, specialized services

#### Escalation Scripts
- **Payment Issue**: "This requires our payment team's attention. Let me connect you with them right away."
- **Policy Dispute**: "This requires our customer relations manager's attention. Let me get them for you."
- **Technical Problem**: "This sounds like a technical issue that needs our IT specialist's attention. Let me connect you with them."
- **Customer Complaint**: "I want to make sure we address your concern properly. Let me connect you with our customer relations manager."

### Example Flows

#### Inbound Order Status
1. **Greet**: "Good {time}, {business_name}. {agent_name} speaking. How may I help you?"
2. **Order**: "I can help you with your order right now. What order number or product are you inquiring about?"
3. **Status**: "Let me check your order status and provide delivery updates."
4. **Updates**: "I'll send you order notifications and tracking information by text."
5. **Support**: "If you have any questions about your order, just call us back."

#### Outbound Product Promotion
1. **Greet**: "Hi {customer_name}, this is {agent_name} from {business_name}. I'm calling about our new {product}."
2. **Product**: "We're offering {specific_product} that I think you'd love. Are you free to hear about it?"
3. **Details**: "The product includes {features} and has received {ratings} from customers. Would you like me to send you the details by text?"
4. **Close**: "I can send you product information and current promotions by text. Does that work for you?"

#### Payment Issue Escalation
1. **Recognize**: "This sounds like a payment processing issue that needs immediate attention."
2. **Escalate**: "This requires our payment team's attention. Let me connect you with them right away."
3. **Support**: "They can provide expert payment assistance and ensure your payment issues are resolved quickly."
4. **Follow-up**: "I'll make sure they follow up with you to ensure your payment is processed successfully."
