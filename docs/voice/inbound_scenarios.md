# Comprehensive Inbound Call Scenarios

## Overview

The voice agent handles comprehensive inbound calls with full feature access, intelligent escalation, and seamless business owner handoff. This document outlines all inbound call scenarios, feature access capabilities, and escalation strategies.

## Primary Inbound Call Categories

### 1. Service Requests

#### Appointment Booking
- **Purpose**: Schedule, reschedule, or cancel appointments
- **Customer Intent**: "I need to book an appointment"
- **AI Response**: "I can help you book an appointment right now. What type of {service} are you looking for?"
- **Features**: Calendar integration, availability checking, time slot selection
- **Follow-up**: Confirmation SMS/email with appointment details

#### Order Management
- **Purpose**: Track orders, check status, modify orders
- **Customer Intent**: "Where is my order?" / "I need to change my order"
- **AI Response**: "I can track your order for you. Do you have an order number, or would you like me to look it up by your phone number?"
- **Features**: Order lookup, status updates, modification requests
- **Follow-up**: Order status notifications

#### Payment Processing
- **Purpose**: Process payments, update payment methods, resolve billing issues
- **Customer Intent**: "I need to pay my bill" / "My payment didn't go through"
- **AI Response**: "I can help you with payment right now. What payment method would you prefer?"
- **Features**: Payment gateway integration, billing system access
- **Follow-up**: Payment confirmation and receipts

### 2. Account Management

#### Profile Updates
- **Purpose**: Update contact information, preferences, account settings
- **Customer Intent**: "I need to update my information"
- **AI Response**: "I can help you update your account information. What would you like to change?"
- **Features**: CRM integration, data validation, security verification
- **Follow-up**: Confirmation of changes made

#### Account Inquiries
- **Purpose**: Check account status, view history, access account details
- **Customer Intent**: "What's my account status?" / "Can I see my history?"
- **AI Response**: "I can help you with your account right now. What specific information do you need?"
- **Features**: Account dashboard access, history retrieval
- **Follow-up**: Account summary via SMS/email

### 3. Support & Troubleshooting

#### Technical Support
- **Purpose**: Resolve technical issues, provide guidance, troubleshoot problems
- **Customer Intent**: "I'm having trouble with..." / "It's not working"
- **AI Response**: "I can help you resolve this issue. Let me walk you through the solution."
- **Features**: Knowledge base access, step-by-step guidance, remote assistance
- **Follow-up**: Issue resolution confirmation

#### Service Complaints
- **Purpose**: Address service issues, resolve complaints, improve satisfaction
- **Customer Intent**: "I'm not happy with..." / "This is unacceptable"
- **AI Response**: "I understand your concern. Let me help you resolve this issue."
- **Features**: Complaint tracking, resolution workflow, satisfaction monitoring
- **Follow-up**: Service recovery and satisfaction check

### 4. Information & Inquiries

#### General Information
- **Purpose**: Provide business information, hours, services, policies
- **Customer Intent**: "What are your hours?" / "Do you offer...?"
- **AI Response**: "I'd be happy to provide that information. What specifically would you like to know?"
- **Features**: Business information database, FAQ access
- **Follow-up**: Information via SMS/email if needed

#### Pricing & Availability
- **Purpose**: Quote prices, check availability, provide estimates
- **Customer Intent**: "How much does it cost?" / "Are you available...?"
- **AI Response**: "I can help you with pricing and availability. What service are you interested in?"
- **Features**: Pricing database, availability checking, quote generation
- **Follow-up**: Detailed quote via SMS/email

## Escalation Scenarios

### Automatic Escalation Triggers

#### Low Confidence Situations
- **Threshold**: AI confidence below 70%
- **Triggers**: Complex questions, technical issues, policy questions
- **Response**: "I want to make sure I give you the most accurate information. Let me connect you with our manager."

#### Customer Requests
- **Triggers**: "I want to speak to a manager", "Can I talk to someone else?"
- **Response**: "Of course, let me connect you with our manager who can assist you properly."

#### Complex Issues
- **Triggers**: Billing disputes, service complaints, special requests
- **Response**: "This sounds like something that needs our manager's attention. Let me get them for you."

#### Compliance Issues
- **Triggers**: Legal questions, regulatory requirements, sensitive topics
- **Response**: "I need to connect you with our compliance specialist for this matter."

### Escalation Handling

#### Immediate Escalation (Business Owner Available)
1. **Acknowledge**: "I want to make sure you get the best help with this."
2. **Transfer**: "Let me connect you with our manager who can assist you properly."
3. **Hold**: Brief hold while connecting
4. **Handoff**: Transfer to business owner

#### Delayed Escalation (Business Owner Unavailable)
1. **Acknowledge**: "I understand this is important."
2. **Explain**: "Our manager is currently unavailable."
3. **Promise**: "I'll make sure they call you back within the next hour."
4. **Schedule**: Set up callback appointment
5. **Follow-up**: "I'll personally ensure this gets resolved."

#### Data Collection for Escalation
- **Basic Information**: Name, phone number, account details
- **Issue Description**: Brief summary of the problem
- **Contact Preferences**: Best time and method to reach them
- **Urgency Level**: How quickly the issue needs to be resolved

## Inbound Call Flow Examples

### Example 1: Appointment Booking
**Customer**: "Hi, I need to book an appointment."
**AI**: "Good morning! I can help you book an appointment right now. What type of service are you looking for?"
**Customer**: "I need a dental cleaning."
**AI**: "Perfect! I can help you schedule a dental cleaning. What day works best for you?"
**Customer**: "Next Tuesday afternoon."
**AI**: "Great! I have Tuesday afternoon available. What time would you prefer, 2 PM or 3 PM?"
**Customer**: "2 PM works."
**AI**: "Perfect! I have you scheduled for Tuesday at 2 PM. You'll receive a confirmation text shortly. Is there anything else I can help you with?"

### Example 2: Order Tracking with Escalation
**Customer**: "Where is my order? It was supposed to be here yesterday."
**AI**: "I can track your order for you. Do you have an order number, or would you like me to look it up by your phone number?"
**Customer**: "The order number is 12345."
**AI**: "Let me check that for you... I see your order is currently in transit and should arrive today. However, I notice there was a delay in processing. Let me connect you with our logistics manager who can provide more detailed information and ensure your order arrives on time."

### Example 3: Payment Issue Resolution
**Customer**: "My payment didn't go through and I'm being charged late fees."
**AI**: "I understand this is frustrating. I can help you resolve this payment issue right now. What payment method would you prefer to use?"
**Customer**: "I want to use my credit card."
**AI**: "I can process your payment with your credit card right now. However, since there are late fees involved, let me connect you with our billing specialist who can review your account and potentially waive those fees for you."

## Business Owner Integration

### Handoff Process
1. **Assessment**: AI evaluates if escalation is needed
2. **Preparation**: Collects relevant customer information
3. **Transfer**: Seamlessly transfers to business owner
4. **Context**: Provides business owner with conversation context
5. **Follow-up**: Ensures issue is resolved

### Follow-up After Resolution
1. **Initiate**: "Hello, this is a follow-up call regarding the issue we discussed."
2. **Update**: "The issue has been resolved on our end."
3. **Verify**: "Is there anything else I can help you with?"
4. **Continue**: Allow for additional conversation if needed

## Best Practices

### Conversation Flow
1. **Warm Greeting**: Use customer name, sound genuine
2. **Permission**: Always ask if they have time
3. **Clear Purpose**: Understand what they need
4. **Feature Access**: Offer relevant services
5. **Engagement**: Ask questions to get them talking
6. **Resolution**: Solve their problem completely
7. **Follow-up**: Ensure satisfaction and offer additional help

### Human-like Interaction
- **Natural Language**: Use conversational, not scripted language
- **Empathy**: Show understanding and concern
- **Active Listening**: Respond to what they say
- **Personalization**: Reference their history and preferences
- **Patience**: Allow time for responses and processing

### Escalation Management
- **Quick Recognition**: Identify when escalation is needed
- **Smooth Transition**: Make handoff seamless
- **Follow-through**: Ensure issues are actually resolved
- **Documentation**: Track all escalations and outcomes
- **Learning**: Improve based on escalation patterns
